Hoops Origin Story

T

he hoops project grew out of my first experience playing basketball in the spring of 2013. As a newcomer I wanted to find new hoops in the city and document the places to ball. New hoops will be added to this collection once-a-week.

John Boilard is a person from Boston, MA who designs for print and the web. John is a basketball enthusiast and the producer of a traveling poster show called The National Poster Retrospecticus.
