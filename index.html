Hoops Origin Story

he hoops project grew out of my first experience playing basketball in the summer of 2013. Only a recent fan of the sport I hadn’t touched a basketball in my entire life. Crazy dribbles, epic blocked shots and the instant report a person builds when playing on a team with total strangers had me geeking out on the game. In an effort to find new courts, document all of the places in the city to ball and visit entirely new neighborhoods this project slowly took off in my spare time. I'd love to see this project extend beyond the city of Boston. Please get in touch if you'd like to rep your city!

John Boilard is a person designer and basketball enthusiast from Boston, Massachusetts. He is also the producer of a traveling poster show called The National Poster Retrospecticus.
